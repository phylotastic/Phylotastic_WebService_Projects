Formica polyctena is a species of European red wood ant in the genus Formica. The pavement ant, Tetramorium caespitum is an ant native to Europe. Pseudomyrmex is a genus of stinging, wasp-like ants. Adetomyrma venatrix is an endangered species of ants endemic to Madagascar. Carebara diversa is a species of ants in the subfamily Formicinae. It is found in many Asian countries.
